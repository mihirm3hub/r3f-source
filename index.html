Accessibility

The Chakan V park is 20 km away from Mumbai-Pune Expressway & offers good connectivity to NH4 Mumbai-Pune National Highway, Pune-Nashik Highway & Pune- Ahmednagar-Aurangabad Highway.

It has great access to neighbouring industrial areas like Ranjangaon,Talegaon.It is also well connected to Pune city,airport & railway station, Chinchwad railway station & JNPT via multi-model transport.
